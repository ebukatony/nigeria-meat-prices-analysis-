Analysis of producer prices for 5 key meats in Nigeria over 17 years, identifying price trends, volatility patterns, and market anomalies.

## Key Findings
1. **Price Hierarchy:** Beef > Sheep > Goat > Chicken > Pork (except 1998 anomaly)
2. **Volatility:** Chicken prices most unstable (short production cycle)
3. **Trend:** All prices showed continuous upward movement
4. **Anomalies:** 
   - Pork price spike in 1998 (normally lowest-priced meat)
   - Chicken spike in 2002
   - Ruminants collective spike in 2008
